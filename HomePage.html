Welcome to Study Hive.

At Study Hive, we’ve created a simple, digital way for students to book seminar rooms, no more running to Ms Black! With just a few clicks, you can check room availability, book your spot, and focus on your studies.

Whether you need a quiet place to study solo or a space for a group project, Study Hive makes it easy as honey to find your perfect spot.

About the Creator

Study Hive was created by Jia Rastogi, a student who wanted to make room booking easier for everyone. As a student myself, I know how important it is to have access to a quiet, dedicated study space when you need it most – so I built this platform to help students like you!
